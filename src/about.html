Meet the team

David & Tonya Kucic

David Kucic served 12 years in the U.S. Army, including two tours at Schofield Barracks. He knows the challenges of PCS moves firsthand · the tight timelines, the worry about schools, the need for a smooth transition. After retiring from active duty, he earned his real estate license and has since helped hundreds of military families find their Hawaii home.

Tonya Kucic grew up in Honolulu and brings 15+ years of local market expertise. She handles negotiations, staging, and marketing with an eye for detail and a heart for service. Together, they make an unstoppable team.
